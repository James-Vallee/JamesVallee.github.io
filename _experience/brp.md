## Vehicle Testing & Instrumentation Support

**Project Summary:** Assisted on-site testing of four-wheeler vehicles, focusing on instrumentation and data validation.  

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Assisted with instrumentation setup.
- Collected and ensure valid test data.
- Supported engineers in ensuring proper operation of test vehicles.

---

## Vibration & Frequency Analysis for Accessories

**Project Summary:** Analyzed vibration and frequency behavior of snowmobile accessories to support engineering specifications.  

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Processed test and simulation data to identify critical frequency ranges.
- Used **nCode** for data analysis and plotting.
- Communicated findings to design engineers for accessory validation.
