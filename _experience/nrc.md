## Overview

I worked as an Aerospace Research Intern within the Structures and Materials Performance group at the National Research Council of Canada. Over the course of a year, I contributed to experimental and analytical research activities supporting aircraft structural performance, materials characterization, and the development of digital twins for aerospace applications.

The role combined hands-on laboratory work, coordination of testing activities, and detailed engineering documentation in a research environment with expectations of publication-quality rigor.

    </p>
